Leadup to 5th Solvay

Planck-----------Einstein

Bohr------------Einstein

Quantum theory began in December 1900. Max Planck started it all when he developed an equation for blackbody radiation. Perplexed himself, the equation only fit the phenomena if the radiative energy was discontinuous, or "quantized". Although revolutionary, Planck was never convinced of the formula's physical reality.

Albert Einstein showed in 1905 - with the photoelectric effect- by displacing electrons from metal, light also behaved as a particle. light particles are known as photons. Niels Bohr, via hydrogen spectra, developed the quantum theory of atomic structure - incorporating Planck/Einstein concepts.
