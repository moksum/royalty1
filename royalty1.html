ELECTRONS and PHOTONS

THE 5TH SOLVAY CONFERENCE







Wasn't just a famous photograph....

...three lectures (a morning and 2 afternoons) fueled a century of quantum debate!!

The universe is governed by two physical laws, the general theory of relativity, and quantum theory. The photo above encompasses the origin of both laws. Einstein is father of relativity, whereas Planck, Einstein, Bohr, deBroglie, Heisenberg, Born, and Schrodinger, are the fathers of quantum theory. ALL are present in the above photo.

The photograph of the physicists who participated in the 5th Solvay Conference has been described as the most intelligent gathering of people in history. 17 of the 29 individuals were recipients of the Nobel Prize, and included such household names as Einstein, Bohr, Curie, Planck, and Heisenberg. So striking was the richness of the company that the photo was equated with an anachronistic gathering of Archimedes, Kepler, Newton, and Galileo. Often overshadowed by the photo, is the profound impact of the few papers that were actually presented. More on the photo. put a link.

conference schedule

Monday 24 October: W.L. Bragg's report

Tuesday 25 October, started 9:00 a.m.: reception.

Tuesday 25 October, afternoon: L. deBroglie's report

Wednesday 26 October, morning: W.Heisenberg's report

Wednesday 26 October, afternoon: E. Schrodinger's report

Thursday 27 October, morning: general discussion (Bohr's contribution)

Friday 28 October, trip to Paris for procession at the Sorbonne.

Saturday 29 October: lunch with the King and Queen of the Belgians.
